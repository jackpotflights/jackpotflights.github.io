About

Jackpot Flights is a premium newsletter service, and it finds good airfare prices through popular search engines, such as Google Flights and Momondo. Most deals appear months in advance of the flight date, so you have the convenience of planning for your trip.

Right now, our deals only depart from some major European cities, and arrive in all the continents except for Europe (and Antartica). If your country has no cities in our departure list and you want it to have, let us know here.

The service is in beta testing phase, so we are using our facebook page to signal the sending of some emails, just in case some subscribers are not receiving them, be it because of them being sent to the spam box or due to any other problem.

We don't get commission for the sales. None of the ticket buying process goes through our website, so we won't send mildly lower prices to your inbox. Our concern is to inform you of the huge discounts only.

"So is it really free?" Yes. Since it is still beta, we don't charge for the service. We want to make sure the best value is being provided before we take payments, and when it happens, all subscribers will be informed.
