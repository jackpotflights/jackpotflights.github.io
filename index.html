About

Jackpot Flights is a premium newsletter service that finds good airfare prices through popular search engines, such as Google Flights and Momondo. Most deals appear months in advance of the flight date, so you have the convenience of planning for your trip.

For now, the flights depart only from some European cities, and arrive in every other continent (except for Antartica). If you want your country in our departure list, let us know here.

Since the service is still in beta testing phase, we use our facebook page to signal the sending of some deals, just in case some subscribers are not receiving the emails due to any problem (like their being sent to the spam box, for example).

We don't get commission for the sales, and none of the ticket buying process goes through our website. Therefore we won't send mildly lower prices to your inbox, only huge discounts.

"But is it really free?" Yes. While the service is beta, we won't charge for the service, so as to make sure it is worth to you.
